Pseudo-code: Daily Tasks Organizer
1. Use LinkedHashMap<Integer, String> tasks

2. Operations:
    - Add task: tasks.put(id, description)
    - Remove task: tasks.remove(id)
    - Display tasks: preserve insertion order

3. Example:
    Add(1, "Check emails")
    Add(2, "Prepare report")
    Add(3, "Call client")
    Remove(2)
    Output: 1->Check emails, 3->Call client
